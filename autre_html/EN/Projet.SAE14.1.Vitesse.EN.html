Speed climbing
In this dicipline the climber must reach the top of the wall as quickly as possible.
there are two official walls, one of 10m and one of 15m. apart from this parameter the speed walls are all identical. They are reputed to be 6B.


The men’s record is held by Indonesian Kiromal Katibin, who climbed the wall in 5.002s last July.


The femminin record is held by the Polish Aleksandra Mirosław, who mounted the wall in 6.53s last May.
